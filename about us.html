🏭 ITI INSTITUTE

📋 Who We Are

Industrial Training Institute (ITI) established in 1985, is a premier vocational training institute committed to shaping skilled professionals for the industry. With 35+ years of excellence, we have produced over 10,000 skilled workers who are contributing to the nation's growth.

Our institute is affiliated to NCVT (National Council for Vocational Training) and recognized by DGET, Government of India. We offer various engineering and non-engineering trades to meet the growing demands of the industry.
